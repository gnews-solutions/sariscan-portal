How to Install on Android

Choose the installation method that fits your device. SariScan is built for fast over-the-air setup so you can start auditing your shelves right away.

📥 1. Sideload via QR / APK

  1. Download APK: Scan the QR code above or tap the "Download Android APK v1.0.0" button to download the package (`app-debug.apk`) directly to your phone.
  2. Open Installer: Once finished, tap on the completed download notification or open your device's File Manager → Downloads folder and tap the APK file.
  3. Allow Sideloading: If prompted that "Your phone is not allowed to install unknown apps from this source", tap Settings right on the popup and toggle on Allow from this source.
  4. Complete Setup: Tap Install on the confirmation modal. Once installed, tap Open to launch `SariScan` instantly with full offline capability!

🌺 2. Huawei AppGallery & HMS

  1. Launch AppGallery: On your Huawei or Honor smartphone, open the pre-installed Huawei AppGallery application.
  2. Search SariScan: Tap the search bar at the top of the store and enter "SariScan Mobile".
  3. Download & Install: Tap the green Install button. AppGallery will automatically verify, download, and install the package onto your device.
  4. HMS Core Ready: Open the app from your home screen. Our native Huawei Mobile Services (HMS Core) integration guarantees that the live camera scanner and local database run smoothly without Google Play Services!

🌌 3. Samsung Galaxy Store & DeX

  1. Open Galaxy Store: On your Samsung smartphone or tablet, launch the official Galaxy Store app.
  2. Install Application: Search for "SariScan Mobile" and tap the download/install icon.
  3. Mobile Shelf Scan: Launch `SariScan` on your phone to audit shelf items with high-performance TFLite GPU acceleration.
  4. Connect to DeX (Desktop Mode): Plug your Galaxy phone via USB-C or HDMI into an external monitor or PC to launch Samsung DeX. `SariScan` automatically shifts into desktop spreadsheet mode, letting you review, edit, and export your Excel/CSV reports with a keyboard and mouse!
